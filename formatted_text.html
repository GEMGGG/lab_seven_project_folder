Getting My First Snake

"I have never feared snakes, because I believe a snake understands when you are his friend, and if he understands you are his friend, he will not bite. - Rudyard Kipling

Looking

I brought my snake home in late 2022...
Getting a snake was a dream I had for many years and After much anticipation my parents finally agreed to let me get one. My dad took my sister and me to a vibrant reptile convention, where we were surrounded by an array of lizards, spiders, and turtles. it was overwhelming to experience such a diverse collection of creatures in one place. Amid all the reptiles, I was drawn to a stunning white snake with a price tag of $2,000. I did consider buying it; However, as a first-time reptile owner I wasn't confident I could provide the care that such a costly pet would require. I continued to explore until I discovered a small,unpretentious snake; his tiny black eyes captivated me, and I felt an immediate connection. Although my dad suggested we could find something more impressive, I was resolved in my choice.


purchasing

As the convention began to wind down, I persuaded my dad to allow me to purchase the snake that had captured my heart. I hurried to the vendor, relieved to find the charming little snake still nestled in a cup. When my dad encouraged me to hold him and I picked him up for the first time and I knew he was the one. This little snake was remarkably affordable at just $40, a much more manageable price compared to the white one. After our purchase, we also acquired a heating pad and a 10-gallon tank to provide him with a suitable home. I was eager to see my moms reaction when I revealed that we had also bought a few baby mice for his meals; her startled shriek was hilarious!

Witnessing his transformation has been truly incredible.
